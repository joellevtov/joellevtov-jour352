Is the Computer Science Department OK?

There are almost no women in the department

By Joel Lev-Tov

If one only looks at the university's data for racial diversity in the Computer Science department, the department seems quite diverse.

But when one digs into the data, one realizes that women are sorely missing in the deparment - both in terms of professors and students.
